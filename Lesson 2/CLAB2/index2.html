Speech Checklist

  1. Content
    • Introduction (stimulate interest)
    • Suffcient information
    • Clear sequence of ideas
    • Summary of main ideas in conclusion
    • Audio-visual aids
    • Pertinent questions:
      • Does the speech simulate and hold interest?
      • Is the speech interesting?
      • Are ideas arranged logically?
      • Is the subject clearly understood?
  2. Delivery
    • Enthusiasm
    • Conversation quality (do not read speech)
    • Rate
    • Correct Grammar
    • Eye Contact
    • Voice (volume and control)
    • Natural expressions and gestures
    • Pleasant facial expressions
    • Self-confidence and poise
    • Personal appearance.
